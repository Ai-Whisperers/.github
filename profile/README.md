# Ai-Whisperers

We build open-source tools and research projects at the intersection of **artificial intelligence** and **scientific computing**.

## Our Focus

- **Machine Learning Research**: Novel architectures and training methodologies
- **Bioinformatics**: AI-powered analysis of biological data
- **Open Science**: Making cutting-edge research accessible to everyone
